WHY WE EXIST

BEFORE GOODBYE

Every day, families walk into shelters carrying the weight of an impossible decision.

Not because they stopped loving their animals.

Because life changed.

  • Housing challenges
  • Unexpected medical bills
  • Financial hardship
  • Limited access to resources

Underdog Co exists in the space before surrender. The moment where compassion, resources, and community can change the ending.
