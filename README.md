Giving underdogs a fighting chance—one family, one shelter, one community at a time.

About

Underdog.CO is a Colorado-based pet resource and apparel brand built around one simple belief:

The best outcome for an animal is almost always staying with the people who love them.

Every day, thousands of pets enter shelters for reasons that have nothing to do with abuse or neglect. Rising housing costs, temporary financial hardship, behavioral challenges, medical expenses, and lack of access to resources force families into impossible decisions.

We're building tools, resources, and products that help keep pets where they belong—at home.

Our Mission

Underdog.CO exists to:

Help keep pets with their families whenever it's safe and possible.
Support responsible, evidence-based animal welfare.
Educate the public about animal behavior and sheltering.
Reduce unnecessary shelter intake through prevention and community support.
Create apparel and merchandise that starts meaningful conversations about animal welfare.

We believe animal welfare can be compassionate and honest.

Our Philosophy

We believe every animal deserves individualized care.

That means recognizing that:

Fear is not aggression.
Behavior changes in shelter environments.
Prevention is often more humane than intervention.
Keeping pets with loving families should be a priority whenever it can be done safely.

We support open conversations about animal welfare, sheltering, behavior, and public policy through evidence, transparency, and respect.

Why Apparel?

Sometimes a T-shirt starts a conversation that statistics never could.

Our designs are created to spark curiosity, challenge assumptions, and encourage people to learn more about animal welfare, behavior, responsible ownership, and the realities shelters face every day.

For Colorado

Underdog.CO was founded in Colorado and is inspired by the incredible people working every day in shelters, rescues, veterinary medicine, animal control, and wildlife care throughout our communities.

A Note

Underdog.CO is a mission-driven business.

We are not a nonprofit.

We believe businesses can create meaningful social impact while building sustainable revenue. Profit allows us to grow, create better educational resources, expand our reach, invest in new projects, and support animal welfare initiatives in ways that are both impactful and long-lasting.

Doing good and building a successful business don't have to be mutually exclusive.
